The Story

In September of 2019 Rick decided to turn his fashion for photography into a full-time business and kicked it off in the
very beginninng of 2020. Bringing the very strong business skills he learned working in the corporate world to bare, he quickly
grew the business to be recognized competitior and well established business in the local market. Along the way adding to his many
skills out of necessity. Rick quickly realized that even if he had the best photography in the world, it wouldn't matter if no one
ever noticed his work. So, he set about trying to find an SEO company to improve his rankings for a Google search. This decision would
eventually lead to the decision to start learnig to code and forming his on web development, SEO and marketing company, DFW Pro SEO.
